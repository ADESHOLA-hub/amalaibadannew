Welcome to Amala Ibadan

AMALA IBADAN

At Amala Ibadan, we pride ourselves on our signature dish – Amala. This traditional Yoruba delicacy, made from yam flour, is expertly prepared to perfection, offering a smooth, hearty texture that pairs beautifully with a variety of rich, flavorful soups. Whether it's Gbegiri, Ewedu, or spicy, slow-cooked stews, each bite is a celebration of heritage and quality.

About Us

A dinstinctive dining destination inspired by culture.

Our menu features a wide array of traditional Nigerian dishes, each crafted with care and the finest ingredients. From Jollof Rice bursting with vibrant tomato flavors to smoky Suya skewers and the comforting warmth of Pounded Yam, we offer a culinary journey through Nigerias diverse regions. Each dish is a reflection of our dedication to preserving the authentic taste and cooking methods that have been passed down through generations.

OUR CEO


Mr. and Mrs. Salako are the visionary leaders behind Amala Ibadan, bringing over four years of experience in the culinary industry. Since taking the helm in 2020, they have driven the company to new heights with their innovative approaches and dedication to excellence. Their leadership has transformed Amala Ibadan into a beloved destination for authentic Nigerian cuisine, blending tradition with modernity while expanding the brands reach and impact. The Salakos are committed to preserving Nigeria's rich food heritage, ensuring every dish is a celebration of culture and quality.
